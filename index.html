Prince Cali is an audio engineer and producer originally from Los Angeles, now based in Portland, Oregon. Producing since 2012, his work blends cinematic atmosphere, hard drums, melody, and emotion into records built to inspire artists rather than follow trends. Every beat is approached with long-term replay value in mind, focusing on sound that feels timeless instead of temporary.
