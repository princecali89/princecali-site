License FAQ

Can I release on Spotify and Apple Music?

Yes, during the active license term.

What happens if my license expires?

If you choose not to renew your license, you are responsible for following the license terms. If required by your agreement, this may include removing your release from streaming platforms, online stores, social media, videos, or any other place where the beat is being used.

Can I make a music video?

Yes, promotional music videos are included during the license term.

Do I own the beat?

No. Ownership remains with Prince Cali unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

Can I renew?

Yes. Renewals are handled directly before or after the term expires.

Can I get an exclusive?

Possibly. Exclusive licensing is not automatic and must be discussed directly.
