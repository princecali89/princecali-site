Built from instinct.

Prince Cali is an independent music producer and audio engineer creating atmospheric hip-hop instrumentals, cinematic textures, and hard-hitting drum-driven records.

A California native with a producer’s ear and an engineer’s attention to detail, Cali focuses on building music that feels polished, emotional, and ready for artists to turn into full records.

His sound blends smooth melodic movement, heavy drums, dark pockets, luxury textures, and clean arrangements made to leave room for vocals while still carrying a strong identity on their own.
